Privacy

Privacy is important to us, no matter how much we argue against it. In the real world, we have been practicing privacy measures but when it comes to digital world, our knowledge is limited. We know less thus we enforce less.

Whatever websites/Apps you would have visited till now, most of them would have told you that they are secure and thus you do not need to worry about privacy. But in reality, privacy is much more vast term than security. Yes. I agree that security is necessary for privacy, but privacy is not security alone. It is much more than that.
